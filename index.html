Paula Dondon

Paula has a degree in Architecture and Urbanism from FAUUSP, and a post-graduate degree in Project Management from Senac University Center. She has worked in urbanism and landscape architecture, firstly at Silvio Soares Macedo's Landscape Architecture Laboratory, than as an intern at Luiz Portugal Arquitetura Paisagística. After that, she worked for seven years at Isabel Duprat's office, where she coordinated residential, institutional, and commercial landscaping projects in São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, and Bahia. Currently, she develops landscaping projetcs at Köber Landschaftsarchitecture, in Stuttgart, Germany.
